CASCADE clean-air-in-schools calculator

Enter a classroom's measured air-quality improvement and read off the economic case for the intervention. Built on the CASCADE decision model (Henderson et al., in preparation).

All figures are in generic monetary units (MU) and use illustrative placeholder parameters, not findings. The point of the tool is to show how a measurement maps to economics once the model's parameters are credibly estimated. The four keystone parameters (κ, σ, π, ρc) are flagged because they are not yet well estimated; drag them to see how much the answer still moves.

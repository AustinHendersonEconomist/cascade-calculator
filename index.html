CASCADE clean-air-in-schools calculator

Enter a classroom's measured air-quality improvement and read off the economic case. A transparent companion to the CASCADE decision model (Henderson et al., in preparation): the equations, every parameter, and the influence structure are shown and clickable.

Figures are in generic monetary units (MU) and use illustrative placeholder parameters, not findings. The four keystone parameters (κ, σ, π, ρc) are flagged because they are not yet well estimated. Click any parameter name below for its definition, baseline sources, and how a study would sharpen it.

The model

$$\mathrm{NSB}_j=\delta\,n\,\bigl(B^{\mathrm{dir}}_j+B^{\mathrm{sec}}_j\bigr)-C_j$$ $$B^{\mathrm{dir}}_j=\underbrace{m\,\kappa\,\sigma_j\,\varphi\,\theta_j}_{\text{averted infections}}\bigl(p_{\mathrm{med}}c_{\mathrm{ch}}+u_{\mathrm{ch}}\lambda+\bar a\,\rho_{\mathrm{day}}\bigr)+\underbrace{g_j\,\rho_{\mathrm{SD}}}_{\text{cognitive}}$$ $$B^{\mathrm{sec}}_j=m\,\kappa\,\sigma_j\,\varphi\,\theta_j\bigl[c_{\mathrm{sup}}+M\textstyle\sum_k\psi_k\bigl(w_ka_k+c_k+u_k\lambda\bigr)\bigr]$$ $$\theta_j=\frac{\eta_j\Delta q_j}{Q_0+\eta_j\Delta q_j}\qquad g_j=\beta\,\Delta\mathrm{CO}_2\,\pi\qquad M=\frac{\rho_c}{1-\rho_c}\qquad C_j=K\cdot\mathrm{CRF}(r,L)+E{+}M$$
